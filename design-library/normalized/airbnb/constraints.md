# airbnb usage constraints

## Replication risk label

- label: high
- rationale: The source brand has very high public recognition, and the single combination of a warm coral red accent, warm near-black text, and a pure white background is itself a strong signature. When the photo-centric listing layout, the circular navigation control, and the three-layer shadow come together, they immediately trigger brand association. The risk is especially high in the lodging and travel domain, where reuse can read as a direct competitive stance.

## Suitable industries

- Experience and hobby booking platforms
- Local shop discovery apps
- Real estate rental platforms
- Lifestyle guide commerce
- Travel-related tools in a category that does not overlap with lodging. For example, flight comparison, local tour guides, travel journal sharing.

## Unsuitable industries

- Lodging-sharing platforms. The direct competitive relationship maximizes replication risk.
- B2B operations dashboard. Excessive decoration hinders task focus.
- Financial trading UI. The warmth slows information-density scanning speed.
- Enterprise admin. Excessive rounding conflicts with task efficiency.
- Security and medical critical control panels. The casual tone damages trustworthiness.

## Tokens that must be replaced when overlaying onto a project

- primary-accent color. Shift `#ff385c` to a different warm hue. Example replacement values are `#ff6b3d` orange-shift, `#e63946` deeper red, and `#ff5a5f` pinker red. Using the original hex as-is immediately triggers brand association.
- display and body fonts. After selecting a substitute candidate for the brand custom VF, choose a font unique to the project. Keeping the weight in the 500–700 range is allowed.
- card shadow 3-layer stack. It may be kept, but rearrange the radius and the combination differently. For example, radius 24px plus a 2-layer shadow dilutes the identity.
- circular 50% nav button. Transform it into a pill (999px) or a rounded square (12px) to dilute the uniqueness.
- photo framing style. Avoid the handheld, natural-light, travel-magazine style and set photo guidelines unique to the project.
- category pill bar horizontal scroll pattern. If needed, replace it with a grid or dropdown to avoid the brand identity.
- 2 premium tiers (`#460479`, `#92174d`). In the original these mark the brand's tier system, so do not borrow them as-is. If the project needs tiers, design them with a completely different palette.
