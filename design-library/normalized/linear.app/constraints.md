# linear.app Usage Constraints

## Clone Risk Label

- Label: med
- Rationale: The combination of the indigo-violet palette and the pure dark stack forms a distinct signature, but Inter (OSS) and the dark background itself are general-purpose elements, so this does not reach the high level.

## Suitable Industries

- Developer tools
- AI governance platforms
- Project management SaaS
- Infrastructure monitoring
- Technical documentation sites

## Unsuitable Industries

- Consumer commerce (reason: an excess of cold tone creates an emotional disconnect)
- Children's education (reason: the dark background lacks accessibility)
- Food service and lifestyle (reason: a tone that is the exact opposite of warm sensibility)

## Tokens That Must Be Replaced When Overlaying a Project

- primary-accent (#5e6ad2): the brand's signature indigo-violet. Replace with the project brand color.
- secondary-accent (#7170ff): a value derived from primary-accent. Replace it together.
- accent-hover (#828fff): the accent hover derived value. Replace it together.
- font-feature-settings ("cv01", "ss03"): OpenType features specific to Inter. When choosing a substitute font, re-review with that font's feature codes.
